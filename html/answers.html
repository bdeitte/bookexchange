how do I post books that I want to sell?

Click on "Add a Book" after you login. Once you post a book, it is immediately viewable by all who visit the site.


how do I find books that I want to buy?

Try a "Search", then you can send a message to the person who has your book. Remember, the book exchange has nothing to do with the actual buying or selling of books. We just provide a convienient way for each side to contact the other.


I sold my book, but people keep asking me about it. How do I stop this?

Login, click on the "Remove" link, and find the book you want to remove.
